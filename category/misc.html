Fire

Published: Mon 26 June 2023
By Matteo Raso

In Misc.

Yesterday, I went on the shortest walk I've been on in months. The smoke from the forest fires not too far from my town was extremely bad, so my eyes began to burn after just a few minutes outside. Growing up, I assumed that we would have figured out global warming before it really began to affect us, but that clearly didn't happen, and now we have massive forest fires because everything is too hot. I hope this will be the wakeup call everybody needs before things get even worse, but I doubt it.
